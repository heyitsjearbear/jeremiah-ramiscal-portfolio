My Story

Aspiring software engineer, aspiring data scientist, aspiring web developer... blah blah blah. To be honest it's all cookie cutter. I'll go further than that.

Growing Up

Growing up, in an Asian-American household, I went through motions, satisying the expectations that my parents set for me. I of course didn't want to dissapoint them, as they had sacrificed so much for my siblings and I to live a comfortable life. In high school, I immersed myself in my studies and extra-curriculars to give myself that edge when it came to college applications. That was fine and all, but in the blink of an eye, everything moved fast. It was almost like, I was trying to grow up fast.

In the present

It would be very unproductive of me to succumb to a "victim's mentality". As of writing this I am pursing my bachelors in Computer Science. So far in my college career, it seems like I have pushed important people in my life away, for the reason of wanting to be successful.
